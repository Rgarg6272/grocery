About Us


OUR STORY SINCE 1975


Girish Kirana & Oil mill was conceived in the year 1975 by Kishorilal and Ramcharan Lal Garg. The Oil mill and Grocery Store are renowned by Kishorilal for his son Ramcharan Lal Garg. Opened in the year 1975, In beginning they faced many problems because of money and place but they didn't give up.

After his Grandfather Passing, his son took over the shop and moved the business forward. They also did many struggle and face a hard problems but Today both of his son Jamuna prasad and Girish together manage the shops and Oil mill also and teaching their children.



THE TRUSTWORTHY GROCERY & OIL STORE


The Journey from a little shop to a big shop is filled with a struggle and hardwork but the owner of our shop Jamuna Prasad & Girish has done it. Starting from the young age 16 because of his father passes away earlier. Born in a poor family, The most important thing is trust which is not easy to gain. From the beginning our products and goods are pure and fresh and we are purely honest with the customer. We are dedicated to customer and Customer satisfaction is an utmost priority for us.
